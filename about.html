TableShop

Born from passion of woodwork and art of the table, TableShop was founded by Robert Nightstand in 1998.
After years of training, he mastered the art of the wood and gave life to his wildest table dreams.
Soon, he and his beloved wife Sarah Workbench welcomed their first and only child to the family table, Peter.
Peter spent his early ages at the workshop, observing and learning from his father in heartwarming dad and son bonding times and he would soon join Robert in this wonderful journey for the perfect table.
Together, they are proud to present you the finest handmade tables, the result of decades of experience in table making.

Robert Nightstand

Robert fell in love for tables early in his life. As he grew up in the woods, he felt a deep connection with this material and quikly wanted to transform this noble material into something higher. That's how he came up with the concept of making a shop for tables.

Peter Nightstand

Heir of the family heritage, Peter keeps innovating and bringing new ideas to his father works. He has already started to make himself a name in the tablemaking community as one of the most genious creator.
